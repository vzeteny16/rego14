# Project Brief

_Created: 2026-05-07_

## One-Sentence Goal

Create a personal, modern, game-like digital birthday gift for Regő that lets him choose a shared brother program with Zeteny and makes the gift feel exciting instead of like a generic coupon.

## Background

- Regő is Zeteny's younger brother.
- Regő turns 14 on 2026-05-09.
- Zeteny wants to give something more meaningful than the usual sweets or small family gift.
- The deeper goal is to build a stronger brotherly relationship.
- Zeteny wants the gift to create shared time, not just give another object.
- A paper coupon feels too simple and weak.
- A digital, interactive version could feel more personal, modern, and fun.

## Current Gift Direction

Create a small web app or page framed as a mission, level, or game dashboard.

The page should let Regő:

- enter or unlock the experience,
- see a birthday mission briefing,
- choose a shared program,
- choose food,
- choose an anime, series, or game option,
- optionally choose a small extra reward,
- see a final confirmation screen.

## What This Is Not

- Not a full life dashboard yet.
- Not a habit tracker yet.
- Not a productivity app for Regő.
- Not a lecture about phone use or bad habits.
- Not a new console purchase.
- Not just a book handed over without context.

## Success Criteria

- Regő understands the gift immediately.
- The first screen feels custom-made for him.
- The page feels modern, exciting, and game-like.
- The choices feel fun, not overwhelming.
- It makes it easy to schedule or choose a shared activity.
- It avoids cringe motivational language.
- It works on phone and desktop.
- It can be finished quickly enough for the birthday.

## Tone

- Brotherly.
- Direct.
- Warm, but not overly emotional.
- Playful, but not childish.
- Modern and slightly game-like.
- No corporate self-improvement language.
